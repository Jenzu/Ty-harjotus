
Battlefield

Battlefield is a series of first-person shooter video games that started out on Microsoft Windows and OS X with its debut video game, Battlefield 1942,
which was released in 2002. The series is developed by Swedish company
EA Digital Illusions CE (DICE) and is published by American company Electronic
Arts. The series features a particular focus on large maps, teamwork and vehicle warfare than most other first-person shooters.
The PC games in the series are mainly focused on online multiplayer.
